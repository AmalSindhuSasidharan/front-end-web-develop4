Pit Bull

These breeds were originally developed as fighting dogs

  • Lifespan: 8 – 15 years
  • Height: 50 cm (Adult)
  • Temperament:
    • Stubborn

    • Obedient

    • Strong Willed

    • Intelligent

    • Courageous

    • Friendly
  • Mass: Male: 16 – 30 kg

Pit bull is the common name for a type of dog. Formal breeds often considered in North America to be of the pit bull type include the American Pit Bull Terrier, American Staffordshire Terrier, American Bully, and Staffordshire Bull
